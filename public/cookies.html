Cookie Policy

Effective Date: June 8, 2025  ·  Last Updated: July 8, 2026

The short version: Lendie is built to be privacy-light. We rely mainly on your browser's local storage (not traditional cookies) to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies, and we do not run analytics on your behavior. A few third-party services we rely on (maps, payments) may set their own cookies.

1. About This Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Lendie LLC ("Lendie," "we," "us") uses cookies and similar technologies (such as browser local storage and session storage) when you use www.lendie.app (the "Platform"). It should be read together with our Privacy Policy, which explains how we handle personal data generally.

2. What Are Cookies and Local Storage?

Cookies are small text files a website stores on your device and sends back to the server on later visits. Local storage and session storage are related browser technologies that let a site save small amounts of data on your device — but, unlike cookies, that data stays in your browser and is not automatically transmitted to a server. Lendie relies primarily on local storage.

3. How Lendie Uses These Technologies

We use browser storage only for strictly necessary and functional purposes — to make the Platform work and to remember your choices. We do not use it for advertising, ad targeting, or cross-site behavioral tracking, and we do not operate third-party analytics on your usage.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some features rely on third-party services that may set their own cookies or use similar technologies. These are controlled by those providers and governed by their privacy and cookie policies, not ours:

  • Google Maps (Google LLC) — powers maps, geocoding, and location search; may set cookies when you use map or location features. See Google's cookie information.
  • Stripe — our payment processor. When card payments are enabled, Stripe sets cookies for fraud prevention and secure payment processing. See Stripe's cookie settings. (Card payments are not enabled by default at all times; Stripe cookies are set only when the payment flow loads.)
  • Supabase — provides authentication and data services; used to keep you signed in.

We do not control these third-party technologies and do not use them to track you across other websites.

5. No Advertising and "Do Not Track"

Lendie displays no advertising and does not sell your personal information or share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. Because we do not track you across sites, "Do Not Track" browser signals have no additional effect on Lendie beyond what we already do.

6. Managing Cookies and Local Storage

You can control or delete cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Common options include blocking all cookies, blocking third-party cookies, or clearing stored site data. Steps vary by browser — see your browser's help pages (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge).

Note: blocking or clearing this data will sign you out and reset your preferences, and may prevent core features from working, because the storage we use is essential or functional rather than optional.

7. Consent

Because the storage Lendie itself uses is strictly necessary or functional — and we do not set optional advertising or tracking cookies — we do not display a cookie-consent banner for our own storage. If we ever introduce analytics or advertising technologies, we will update this policy and provide an appropriate consent or opt-out mechanism before doing so.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here and revise the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Platform after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
